Here at Video Universe, we absolutely LOVE movies.

As one of the last brick & mortar video stores in the country, we believe that in-person movie rental is an experience that is too valuable to lose, and we pride ourselves on our ability to continue to bring the experience to our community. Some of our fondest memories as lifelong cinema fans involve walking up and down the aisles of our local mom & pop video shop, finding a movie title or cover art that grabs us, and building weekend plans around watching our selections. That nostalgia is so important to us that we hope to pass along the beauty of that experience along to you. Sure, you could just plop down on your couch and scroll endlessly through Netflix, but we believe that there is real magic in flipping through slim DVD cases to find hidden gems or rummaging through stacks of VHS tapes that you simply won't find on any streaming platform. Plus, we offer a variety of your favorite movie concessions conveniently located right next to our checkout counter.
